Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

1 Information We Collect

When you interact with Olivintra, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address provided during checkout.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Razorpay. We do not store your card details on our servers.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited to improve your experience.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your shopping experience and remember your preferences.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide you with the best shopping experience:

  • Process and fulfill your orders accurately
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and delivery notifications
  • Improve our products, services, and website functionality
  • Respond to your inquiries, complaints, and feedback
  • Send promotional offers and updates (only with your explicit consent)

3 Information Sharing

We value your trust and never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only when necessary:

  • Payment Processors: Razorpay for secure payment processing
  • Delivery Partners: To fulfill and deliver your orders
  • Legal Authorities: When required by applicable laws or to protect our rights

4 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL Encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with firewalls and monitoring
  • Razorpay's PCI DSS compliant payment gateway
  • Regular security audits and updates

⚠️ While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5 Your Rights

You have full control over your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications anytime

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

6 Cookies Policy

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your shopping cart items
  • Save your preferences for future visits
  • Analyze website traffic and improve user experience
  • Provide personalized product recommendations

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as Razorpay and WhatsApp). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the effective date clearly mentioned. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
