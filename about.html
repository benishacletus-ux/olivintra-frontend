About Olivintra

Premium Ethnic Wear Crafted with Love in Kerala

Our Story

Founded in 2026 in the culturally rich town of Parassala, Kerala, Olivintra was born from a deep passion for ethnic fashion and a commitment to quality craftsmanship.

We believe that every woman deserves to feel elegant, confident, and beautiful in her attire. Our collections are carefully curated, blending traditional Kerala craftsmanship with contemporary designs.

From luxurious silk kurtis to elegant sarees, each piece is crafted with attention to detail and premium quality fabrics, ensuring you look your best for every occasion.

Our Vision

To become Kerala's most trusted ethnic wear brand, known for quality, authenticity, and timeless elegance.

Our Mission

  • Deliver premium quality ethnic wear at honest prices
  • Celebrate Kerala's rich textile heritage through modern designs
  • Provide exceptional customer service and seamless shopping experience
